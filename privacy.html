Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 3, 2026

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Skuba (operated by Lazuli Global), we believe your privacy is fundamental. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Skuba mobile application.

Key principle: Skuba works 100% offline. We don't collect personal data unless absolutely necessary, and we never sell your information.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: If you create an account, we collect your email address
  • Dive Plans: All dive plans, gas calculations, and settings are stored locally on your device
  • Support Requests: When you contact support at hello@skuba.app, we collect the information you provide

Automatically Collected Information

  • Crash Reports: If the app crashes, we may collect anonymous crash logs to fix bugs
  • Analytics (Optional): With your consent, we may collect usage statistics to improve the app (e.g., which features are used most)
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version (for compatibility and support)

What We DON'T Collect

  • ❌ Your dive logs (stored locally only)
  • ❌ Your location or GPS data
  • ❌ Your personal identification beyond email
  • ❌ Your contacts or photos
  • ❌ Browsing history or third-party app usage

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Provide the Service: Enable app functionality and sync (if opted in)
  • Improve the App: Fix bugs, add features, and optimize performance
  • Customer Support: Respond to your questions and issues
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws and regulations

We never: Sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it for unrelated purposes.

Data Storage & Security

Local Storage

Skuba is designed as an offline-first application. Your dive plans, calculations, and settings are stored locally on your device. We cannot access this data unless you explicitly share it with us (e.g., for support).

Cloud Backup (Optional)

If you enable cloud backup, your dive plans are encrypted and stored securely. Only you can access this data with your account credentials.

Security Measures

  • Industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited access to user data (only authorized personnel)

Note: While we implement strong security measures, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but work diligently to protect your information.

Third-Party Services

Skuba may use third-party services for specific functions:

  • App Stores: Apple App Store and Google Play Store (subject to their privacy policies)
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage analytics (if you opt in) via privacy-focused providers
  • Crash Reporting: Anonymous crash logs to identify and fix bugs

These services have their own privacy policies. We choose providers that respect user privacy and minimize data collection.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-Out: Disable analytics or marketing communications at any time
  • Data Portability: Export your dive plans and settings

To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@skuba.app. We will respond within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Skuba is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at hello@skuba.app.

International Users

Skuba is operated by Lazuli Global and is available worldwide. If you access Skuba from outside your country of residence, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in different jurisdictions. We comply with applicable data protection laws in all jurisdictions where we operate.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page
  • Displaying an in-app notification (for major changes)
  • Sending an email notification (if you have an account)

Your continued use of Skuba after changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal information, please contact us:

  • Email: hello@skuba.app
  • Company: Lazuli Global
  • Website: skuba.app

We will respond to all legitimate requests within 30 days.

Summary in plain English: Skuba works offline. We don't collect your dive data. We only collect what's necessary to make the app work and fix bugs. We never sell your information. You can delete your account anytime. Questions? Email hello@skuba.app.
